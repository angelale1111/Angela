About

Dr. Angela Le

Dr. Le has spent twenty-five years bridging the science of medicine and the art of living fully.

She came to this work through her own healing. A transformative experience in India led her to the study of Traditional Chinese Medicine and Taoist philosophy, within a lineage stretching back 74 generations. She has been in practice since 2001.

She has worked with thousands of people across the full spectrum of human experience, from reproductive challenges to intimacy, purpose, and the deepest questions of what it means to be alive. Over time her patients brought her to the limits of what medicine alone could offer, and that is where her real work began.

She leads the global community of nonduality teacher David Bingham and is collaborating with him on Effortless Love, a book about what becomes possible when we stop seeking and rest in what we already are.

She believes every human being is already whole. The work is remembering.

Everything she teaches, she has embodied first.

The Work

Authorship

A life lived from the inside out.

You are living a version of life that was handed to you. The beliefs, the patterns, the quiet narrowing of what feels possible. Somewhere along the way you stopped asking whether any of it was actually yours.

There is more available to you than you have yet created. More aliveness. More ease. More vitality in every area of your life. It is not far away. Something has simply been standing in the way.

Here, you are met. And this is where the life you have been waiting to live begins.

This work goes there. Into the depth of what you are capable of. Into what becomes possible when you stop living from the script you were given.

Offerings

Four Pathways. One Foundation.

Each One Meets You Where You Are
01

Health Optimization

Full ownership of your health. From sleep and hormonal balance to detoxification and longevity, this pathway gives you the tools and understanding to become the authority on what your body is asking for. This is where sovereignty begins.

02

Somatic Healing

Your nervous system holds everything you have experienced. This work goes there directly, releasing what has accumulated and restoring what was always yours. What opens on the other side is more aliveness, more ease, more capacity to receive.

03

Effortless Living

You have done the healing work. The deeper question now is who you are without the conditioning, what becomes available when you stop carrying what was never yours. This is where effort drops away and life begins to move through you.

04

Community

You were not meant to do this work alone. This is a private space for aligned women to practice, connect, and grow together. The quality of who surrounds you shapes everything you create. This space holds that possibility.
